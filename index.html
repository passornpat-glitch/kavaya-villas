About Kavaya

Where Space
Becomes Serenity

Welcome to KAVAYA Na Tai — an intimate collection of seven private residences set within Na Tai's quiet coastal landscape.

Each home is designed as a single-level sanctuary — open, effortless, and grounded in nature. Architecture sits low and horizontal, embracing gardens, light, and air.

Private courtyards, plunge pools, and herb gardens create a rhythm that feels personal and unhurried. Barrier-free planning and thoughtful circulation ensure comfort not only for today, but for the years ahead.

About The Founders

Vision, Heritage &
Long-Term Perspective

KAVAYA Na Tai is led by a husband-and-wife partnership grounded in experience, vision, and long-term perspective.

Nam has been active in Phuket's property sector since 2005, contributing to some of the island's early residential developments. After dedicating time to raising her family, she returns to development with a renewed purpose — to create a private sanctuary defined by calm architecture, thoughtful planning, and enduring quality.

Her husband brings international entrepreneurial experience across technology and mobility sectors, strengthening the project with strategic clarity and operational discipline.

Together, they combine local heritage with global perspective — crafting homes designed for longevity, privacy, and meaningful living.
